The Static Echo — Live at The Black Bear

Open
Sat 19 Apr 2025 8:00 PM — 11:30 PM Black Bear Lodge, Fortitude Valley Indie Rock Posted by Alex Turner

About This Event

The Static Echo return to The Gasometer for a night of raw, reverb-drenched indie rock. Expect tight guitar work, melodic hooks, and the kind of energy only a Collingwood basement show can produce. Support comes from local acts Pale Grey Horses and Foxfield.

Doors open at 7:30 PM. All ages welcome. Bar on site. Limited capacity — grab your ticket early.
